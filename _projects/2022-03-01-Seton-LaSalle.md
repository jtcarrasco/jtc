## Scope
[Seton LaSalle Catholic High School](https://www.slshs.org/) is a private co-educational institution in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, formed through the merger of two historic Catholic schools. The school serves students in grades 9-12 with a focus on faith-based education, academic excellence, and athletic achievement.
