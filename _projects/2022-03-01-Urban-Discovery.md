## Scope
Urban Discovery Schools is a charter school organization in San Diego, California, operating in partnership with UC San Diego. The school emphasizes project-based learning and STEAM education, offering a unique academic model that connects students with university resources and real-world research opportunities.

### Role & Approach
At Edlio, I collaborated with a project manager and designer to deliver Urban Discovery Schools' digital platform, serving as the lead developer responsible for the site's technical implementation. The project centered on communicating the school's distinctive partnership with UC San Diego and its innovative approach to education.
